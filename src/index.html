ABOUT ME

I am what they call a Nashville unicorn. Being born and raised in Nashville has now become one of my claims to fame. I have always felt that using and utilizing technology is like second nature to me, and I believe that many different problems or issues can be solved using technology. I realized that in becoming a software developer, I will be able to utilize the skills I have developed as a teacher and will allow me to turn something that was more of a hobby into a career in which I could grow and develop overtime. After choosing to attend Nashville Software School to begin my new career in software development, it became increasingly clear that this was the right field for me. I am excited about my future as a software developer because I know it will allow me to help others solve problems or make their lives more efficient, to think critically about the way something works and be able to adapt it to meet the needs of the consumer or client, to be creative, and to grow individually while also having opportunities to collaborate with colleagues to improve outcomes. I have enjoyed challenging my brain to think in new ways. With web development, there are endless possibilities! When I am not working, I enjoy playing outside with my twin boys, cooking, traveling, binge watching series on Netflix, and exploring new restaurants.
